# Privacy Policy for Bounce Craft

**Last Updated: March 23, 2026**

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to **Bounce Craft**. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all our users, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

**Bounce Craft is designed to be safe for children under the age of 13.**

## 2. Information We Collect

### a. Personal Information
**We do not collect any personally identifiable information.** We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other personal details.

### b. Local Settings
The app saves your preferences (such as shape types, theme mode, and game limits) locally on your device using Android DataStore. This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

### c. Automated Data Collection (Google Play)
Since Bounce Craft is distributed through the Google Play Store, Google may automatically collect certain non-personal information, such as:
* Device model and operating system version.
* App crash logs and performance diagnostics.
* App installation and update events.

This data is collected by Google to help improve the stability and performance of the Android ecosystem. For more information, please refer to [Google’s Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 3. External Links and Third-Party Services
Bounce Craft contains a link to a third-party service (**Buy Me a Coffee**) to allow users to support the developer. 

* **Data Collection by Third Parties:** If you click on this link, you will be directed to a third-party website. We do not control and are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites. They have their own privacy policies and may collect personal information (such as payment details) if you choose to interact with them.
* **Parental Guidance:** We encourage parents to supervise their children's use of the app and to be aware when they are leaving the app to visit an external site. For an optimal child-safe experience, we recommend that parents handle any external interactions.

## 4. Use of Permissions
Bounce Craft requests the following permission:
* **Access Notification Policy**: This is used solely to provide the "Disable Notifications" feature, allowing for an uninterrupted play experience. It does not access your personal messages or notification content.

## 5. Third-Party Services and Ads
* **No Third-Party Analytics**: We do not use third-party tracking or analytics SDKs within the app.
* **No Advertising**: Bounce Craft does not contain any advertisements.
* **No Social Media Integration**: The app does not use social media SDKs or "sign-in" features.

## 6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)
We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). 
* We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under 13.
* If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

## 7. Data Security
Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information stored on our servers to protect. Your game settings are stored securely on your own device.

## 8. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
